Our Commitment

Alegre Solutions GS is committed to making alegresolutionsgs.com usable by as many people as possible, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA where feasible.

What We've Built In

  • Visible keyboard focus states on all interactive elements
  • Semantic heading structure and skip-to-content link
  • Alt text on meaningful images
  • Sufficient color contrast against our dark background
  • Reduced-motion support for users with that browser/OS preference
  • Accessible labels on form fields and the cookie consent banner

Ongoing Work

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time fix. We periodically review the Site and welcome feedback on any barriers you encounter.

Feedback

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of this Site, please contact us at info@alegresolutionsgs.com or (725) 220-3324 and we will work to address it promptly.
