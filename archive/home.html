About Alegre Solutions GS

Built by someone who did it first.

The owner of Alegre Solutions GS didn't start in marketing. He started as a commercial electrician - blue collar, hands-on, working job sites.

He quit his job and started a window cleaning business from scratch. No marketing playbook, no agency - just figuring it out. That business grew, and so did the hunger to scale. He went on to acquire and operate three home service businesses across different markets, learning through trial and error what actually moves revenue in the trades.

The answer wasn't more leads. It was a system: speed-to-lead, consistent follow-up, a CRM that tracked everything, and a sales process that closed. Once that system was in place, growth became predictable.

Now the mission is simple: install that same system for home service contractors who are ready to scale - and stop leaving jobs on the table.
