How Many Lead Sources Does Your Business Actually Need?

AS
Alegre Solutions GS Team · May 22, 2026

A lot of contractors run their entire pipeline through a single source - one ad platform, one directory, one referral relationship. It works, until it doesn't.

We typically aim for three to five active lead sources for a contractor client: usually a mix of paid social, search-based demand, and a referral or repeat-customer channel. The goal isn't volume for its own sake - it's not being one platform change away from an empty month.

Start with the offer, not the channel

Before adding a new lead source, we get clear on what makes the business worth choosing in the first place. A strong offer performs across more channels with less spend than a weak one running everywhere.
