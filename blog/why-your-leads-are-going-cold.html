Why Your Leads Are Going Cold (And It's Not the Ads)

AS
Alegre Solutions GS Team · June 12, 2026

When a contractor tells us their ads "stopped working," the first thing we check isn't the campaign - it's the stopwatch. How long does it take for a new lead to get a text back?

In most of the pipelines we audit, the answer is hours, not seconds. By the time someone responds, the homeowner has already called the next name on their list. The ad did its job. The follow-up didn't.

The 60-second window

Contractors who respond to a new lead within 60 seconds close meaningfully more jobs than those who wait even a few minutes. That's not a marketing stat - it's a behavior stat. People requesting an estimate are usually comparing three or four companies at once, and the first one to respond gets the benefit of the doubt.

What actually fixes it

Automated text response, a structured nurture sequence over the following 7-14 days, and a clear no-show recovery process. None of it requires more ad spend - it requires the system being built before the ad budget gets increased.
