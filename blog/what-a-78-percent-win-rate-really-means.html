What a 78% Win Rate Really Means for Your Business

AS
Alegre Solutions GS Team · June 3, 2026

You've probably seen the stat: the company that responds first to a lead wins the job a large majority of the time. It's easy to nod along and move on. It's more useful to do the math on your own business.

If you're running ten estimates a month and losing even three of them simply because a competitor called back first, that's three jobs a month - every month - left on the table before your sales process even started.

Speed is a sales advantage, not a tech feature

Treat response time as part of your sales process, not an IT setting. The estimate you give still has to be good. But you can't sell a job you never got invited to quote.
